About

My Story Today

Hi, I'm a UI/UX Designer with a PhD in Design from the Faculty of Fine Arts. My diverse experience and passion led me to specialize in UI/UX design; as I have a background that blends Interior Architecture, Visual digital design, research/analysis, sales/marketing and managing groups of Junior designer / students.

Given my background, my transition to UX/UI design felt natural, where human-centered ingenuity is at its core. I graduated with honors (second of my graduation class) as a hard worker with an analytical problem-solving mentality. I have worked as a UI/UX freelancer and enjoyed working with a team and with clients analyzing user centered design requirements.

I am preparing for a “Front End Design” Certificate to enhance my ability to assist and work with developers, while designing prototypes. I want to make a difference in creating efficient seamless aesthetical user centered experiences that help market products.
